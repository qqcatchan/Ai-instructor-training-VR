📋 Project Overview

Music and Song Training VR is an integrated platform combining three powerful technologies:

🥽 VR Training

Immersive virtual reality environment for instructor training and activity-based learning

🎼 Music Generation

Automatic music generation and processing for training scenarios

🤖 AI Instructor

Intelligent AI-powered instructor system for personalized training

✨ Key Features

  • 🎓 AI Instructor Training System - Advanced training mechanisms with error detection and recovery
  • 🏃 Multi-Modal Training - Motion, Music, and Speech training modules
  • 🔍 Flash Check Verification - AI-powered content validation against authoritative resources
  • 💾 Progress Tracking - Automatic saving and recovery of training sessions
  • 🌐 VR/XR Integration - Full virtual and extended reality support

🆕 Latest Features (2026-06-12)

✅ Integrated AI Training Modules

  • Motion Training: Real-time pose detection with MediaPipe
  • Music Training: Audio frequency analysis and pitch detection
  • Speech Training: Multi-language speech recognition support
  • Authoritative Resources: Access to certified training materials from trusted sources
  • AI Content Generation: Intelligent alternative content creation when resources unavailable
  • Error Recovery: Automatic crash detection and session recovery
